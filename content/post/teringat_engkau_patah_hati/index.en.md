The sun was rising in the morning; plants began to eagerly bloom. I remembered this morning heading for deep submission. It's refreshing to experience, even though brief, despite the intense radiation from the sun.

I won't forget the time when the past happened and created fleeting memories. This heart feels very unwilling and rebellious to engage in romance. I direct these thoughts to you who have filled this fragile heart.

The flowing water in the stream is sincerely validating deep emotions, even though they're hard to forget. I've tried my hardest to let go of myself in this worldly shadow, to find a place where you declare comfort and happiness just for yourself.

The voice I remember from that time and our laughter, this heart is open to receive you with a very sincere statement. But I don't blame you for any of it; it's just a voice in my heart wanting to express a word.

The last smile I saw as we struggled to build a future, I remember it all. Time and periods have changed, drifting further and further apart, where you filled this fragile heart, so heavily I remember it all.

I don't want all these memories to become a burden in the future. This spring cannot deceive the feelings I experience now, even though it can only reminisce. There are endless limitations, red roses falling in these tears.

I have lived life slowly. Day by day, I want to hope that you can change me within my dark circle.
